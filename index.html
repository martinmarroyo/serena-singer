A fusion pop artist born and bred in Brooklyn. My music combines the beloved melodies of 90s icons like Celine Dion and Sade with a modern twist, creating a unique sound that pushes the boundaries of pop music. I honed my singing skills at the American Musical and Dramatic Academy and have been recognized locally for my performances and hosting roles. In addition to my artistic career, I have a business degree, triumphed in singing competitions and I've even performed for the President of Sicily! I'm currently writing new music that blends my eclectic influences with fresh ideas.
